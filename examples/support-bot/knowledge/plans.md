# Plans & Pricing

## Tiers

### Starter — $19/month
- 3 seats
- Core features only
- Email support (response within 1 business day)
- 5 GB storage
- 10,000 API calls per month
- No SSO, no SAML, no custom roles

### Pro — $89/month
- 15 seats
- Core + workflows + integrations
- Priority support (response within 4 business hours, 24/5)
- 100 GB storage
- 1,000,000 API calls per month
- Webhooks, custom fields, basic SSO (Google, Microsoft)
- 30-day free trial
- 15% discount on annual billing

### Enterprise — custom pricing
- Unlimited seats
- Everything in Pro + dedicated CSM, SLA, custom contracts
- 99.95% uptime SLA
- Advanced SSO (Okta, OneLogin, SAML), SCIM provisioning, custom roles
- Audit logs (90-day retention)
- Single-tenant deployments available
- Procurement & legal review supported (DPA, BAA available)

## Switching plans

Upgrade: effective immediately, prorated for the current billing period.

Downgrade: effective at next renewal. Existing data is preserved; some features may become read-only (e.g., workflows on a downgrade from Pro to Starter).

Cancellation: takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You retain access until then; data is exported automatically and emailed to the billing contact.

## Add-ons

- **Extra seats** — $8/month on Starter, $5/month on Pro, included on Enterprise.
- **Extra storage** — $20/month per 100 GB on Starter/Pro; included on Enterprise.
- **Premium support add-on** — $200/month, available on Pro. Adds a Slack-shared channel and 1-hour response SLA.
