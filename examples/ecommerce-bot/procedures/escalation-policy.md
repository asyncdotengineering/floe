# Escalation Policy

Escalate to a human agent when:

1. The customer explicitly asks ("can I talk to someone").
2. The customer threatens legal action, chargeback, or public escalation (social media, reviews).
3. The customer has had a refund denied and remains unsatisfied after one alternative offer.
4. The conversation has exceeded 8 turns without resolution.
5. Any compliance-sensitive issue (data deletion request, dispute, fraud claim).

When escalating, briefly summarize the issue for the human agent and acknowledge the wait to the customer. Never disclose internal escalation reasons verbatim — use neutral language: "I'm connecting you with a team member who can help further."
