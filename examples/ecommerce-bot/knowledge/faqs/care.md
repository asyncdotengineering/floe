# Care FAQ

- **Down jackets**: machine wash cold, tumble dry low with 2-3 tennis balls to restore loft. Spot-clean stains immediately.
- **Leather goods (sneakers, totes)**: condition every 6 months with a neutral leather cream. Avoid prolonged sun exposure.
- **Pima cotton tees**: machine wash cold, tumble dry low. Pre-washed so shrinkage is minimal.
- **Merino scarves**: hand wash cool with wool detergent; lay flat. Never machine wash merino.

If a care label conflicts with this guide, follow the label.
