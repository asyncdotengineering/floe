# Shipping

- **Standard shipping**: $7 flat, 5-7 business days. Free for orders over $75.
- **Express**: $18 flat, 2 business days. Available to continental US only.
- **International**: $25 base + customs handling. Allow 7-14 days plus customs clearance.

Orders placed before 1 pm Eastern ship the same day. Saturday and Sunday don't count as business days.

Address changes can be requested before the order enters "Shipped" status — after that you'll need to refuse delivery and request a return.

We don't ship to: PO boxes (for items over $50), APO/FPO addresses (carrier limits).
