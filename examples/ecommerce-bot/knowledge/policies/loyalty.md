# Acme Loyalty

- **Earn**: 1 point per $1 spent on full-price items. Sale items earn 0.5 points per $1.
- **Redeem**: 100 points = $5 off any order. Stackable up to $50.
- **Tiers**:
  - Bronze (0-499 pts): standard shipping discounts on orders over $50
  - Silver (500-1999 pts): free standard shipping on every order, early sale access
  - Gold (2000+ pts): free express shipping, dedicated concierge line, annual gift item

Points expire 24 months after the last purchase. Tier status is recalculated annually each January.
