# Acme Pricing

## Free
- Up to 3 users
- 5 GB storage
- Community support only

## Pro — $12/user/month
- Unlimited users
- 100 GB per user
- Email support, 24-hour response
- All integrations (Slack, GitHub, Jira, Linear)
- Video calls up to 25 participants

## Enterprise — custom
- SSO, SCIM, audit logs
- Custom data residency
- Priority support, 1-hour response
- Dedicated customer success manager
- Custom contracts and DPA available

Annual billing receives a 17% discount on Pro and Enterprise.
